#  VendorBridge — Procurement & Vendor Management ERP

A modern **enterprise-grade procurement platform** that automates vendor onboarding, RFQ workflows, quotation comparison, approvals, purchase orders, invoices, contracts, notifications, and analytics through a centralized dashboard.

---

```text
VENDOR MANAGEMENT
        ↓

        ↓
VENDOR ASSIGNMENT
        ↓
QUOTATION SUBMISSION
        ↓
QUOTATION COMPARISON
        ↓
MULTI-LEVEL APPROVAL
        ↓
PURCHASE ORDER
        ↓

        ↓
ANALYTICS & INSIGHTS
```
